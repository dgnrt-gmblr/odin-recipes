Air Fryer Lemon Pepper Chicken Wings

Easy lemon pepper wings made in the air fryer so you can eat them microwave often without the guilt of deep-frying. Tangy and bold flavour.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon kewpie mayo (regular works as well)
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon lemon-pepper seasoning
  • 1.5lb chicken wings

Directions

  1. Toss the chicken wings with garlic powder, black pepper and cumin. Add kewpie mayo and a squeeze of lemon. Let marinade for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat air fryer to 390C and fry the wings for 18 minutes, flipping half way through.
  3. Squeeze rest of the lemon over hot wings and toss with the lemon-pepper seasoning
